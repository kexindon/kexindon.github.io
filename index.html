About Me

I’m a first‑year PhD student in Biology at MIT, focusing on genetics and tumor evolution. My research combines CRISPR screens, single‑cell sequencing and machine learning to unravel how cancer adapts under therapeutic pressure.

Beyond the lab, I love capturing urban moments and natural light with my Fujifilm camera. Photography helps me keep a keen eye for detail—a skill that benefits both science and art.

Research Interests

  • Cancer functional genomics & tumor heterogeneity
  • CRISPR / Base‑ & Prime‑editing technologies
  • Single‑cell multi‑omics
  • Machine learning for genotype‑phenotype mapping
